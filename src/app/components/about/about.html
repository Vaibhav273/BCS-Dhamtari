Welcome to BCS Collage

Realizing the need for a college for the development of higher education in Dhamtari region, Adarsh Shikshan Samiti Dhamtari established Nutan Arts and Commerce College in 1963. Due to lack of facilities for studying science in Nutan College, Lal Bahadur Shikshan Samiti Dhamtari established Dhamtari Science and Arts and Commerce College in 1966-67. Postgraduate Economics classes started in Nutan College in 1972-73. To rescue these two institutions from financial crisis, Municipal Council Dhamtari acquired these two colleges on 01 January 1980. Later, the Higher Education Department of M.P. Government acquired these colleges on 09 June 1981 and integrated them. Thus, Government College Dhamtari came into existence. The college campus is spread over 27.70 acres. In December 1995, the college was given the status of a postgraduate college.

To keep the memory of Babu Chhotelal Srivastava, the famous freedom fighter of the region, intact, the government named the college after him in 1995. Now this college is known as Babu Chhotelal Srivastava Government Post Graduate College, Dhamtari. The college has the facility of postgraduate studies in Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Zoology, Information Technology, Home Science, Commerce, Economics, Political Science, Geography, Hindi, History, Botany, English and Sociology. Two research centers are running in the college in Hindi and Economics subjects respectively. Indira Gandhi National University is also running in the college. The study center of Pt. Sundarlal Sharma Open University Chhattisgarh, Bilaspur is running in the college from 13.12.2012. With the cooperation of Jan Bhagidari Samiti and the government, development work is going on in the college for the convenience of the students.
